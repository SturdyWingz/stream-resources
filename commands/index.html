Gambling

Wingletz
!addwingletz <username> <amount> Mod only

Adds or removes wingletz to/from a user. Example: !addwingletz Argon014 100 To remove wingletz, use a negative number as the amount.

!wingletz

Displays your current number of wingletz.

!claim

Every 12 hours you can claim 250 free wingletz on me. I am extremely generous, I know.

!giftwingletz <username> <amount>

Feeling generous? Gift some of your wingletz to another twitch user! Example: !giftwingletz EclipseMoon2009 100

!gamble <amount>

Gamble your wingletz in a 50/50 win chance! Some say rigged, I say probably not :) Example: !gamble 100

!gamble winloss

Check your current wins/losses for your gambling addiction.

!slots <amount>

Gamble your wingletz with higher risk and higher potential rewards! Example: !slots 100

!slots winloss

Check your current wins/losses for your slots addiction.
